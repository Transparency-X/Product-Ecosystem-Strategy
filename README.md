## 1. Overview & Mission

Transparency X’s Core Index Framework (XCIF) already defines a new category of sociotechnical measurement. But the infrastructure, standards, and community we’re building can fuel far more than indexes.

This repository is the **strategic research hub for Transparency X’s business operations, business development, and product ecosystem design**. It contains:

- A comprehensive **product portfolio analysis** identifying 16 high-value product groups — each rigorously assessed for compatibility, mission alignment, and commercial potential.
- A clear **taxonomy** distinguishing products that *extend* the core index ecosystem from those that are *separate, parallel ventures*.
- A **roadmap** for phased exploration, validation, and launch, building on XCIF’s existing technical capabilities.

We’re not just measuring invisible harm. We’re building a sustainable, diversified organisation that can outlast any single metric, regulation, or funding cycle — while accelerating the shift toward a world where human autonomy is protected by design.

## 4. Features of This Research

This repository is designed as a **living strategic asset**, not a static PDF. Key features:

- **Structured Decision Framework**  
  Each product group is scored against six dimensions: *Mission Alignment, Technical Leverage, Market Urgency, Revenue Potential, Regulatory Tailwinds, and Ecosystem Synergy*. This allows transparent prioritisation as conditions change.

- **Twin-Lens Taxonomy**  
  Products are explicitly tagged as *Ecosystem Extenders* or *Parallel Ventures*, preventing category confusion and helping allocate resources appropriately.

- **XCIF Engineering Anchor**  
  Every extender product maps directly to specific XCIF components (ingestion APIs, QA CI/CD, Dynamic Weighting Engine), minimising duplicated effort.

- **Ethics & Risk Pre-Mortems**  
  Each product canvas includes a weaponisation assessment, privacy impact analysis, and exit strategy, ensuring we never build something that contradicts our core mission.

- **Executable Roadmaps with Maturity Gates**  
  Phases are tied to concrete milestones from the XCIF roadmap (e.g., “requires L4 Predictive indexes”) and external regulatory events (e.g., “post-EU AI Act enforcement”).

- **Open, Forkable Governance**  
  The research is licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0. External strategists, academics, and community members can fork, critique, and remix the portfolio strategy just like our code.

## 5. Roadmap: From Strategy to Execution

We sequence our product development in four horizons, aligned with XCIF’s own maturity phases and market readiness. This is not a rigid gantt chart; it’s a living priority stack.

### Horizon 0 — Foundation (Now – Q3 2026)
*Anchors the strategy in rigorous documentation and small-scale validation.*

- [x] Complete initial Product Portfolio Map (this repo).
- [ ] Publish detailed canvases for all 16 product groups.
- [ ] Launch **Sensor Network Pilot** for ACI in one municipality.
- [ ] Prototype the **TIE** enterprise dashboard with one early-adopter client.
- [ ] Spin up **Foundation shell** and apply for initial research grants.

### Horizon 1 — Early Revenue & Proof (Q4 2026 – Q2 2027)
*Launches the first monetisable products that require minimal new infrastructure.*

- [ ] Launch **Data Feeds & Real-Time Streams** (v1.0) for commercial subscribers.
- [ ] Open **Certification & Trustmark Program** for third-party indexes.
- [ ] Release **Education & Training** pilot (online course + TX Auditor badge).
- [ ] Initiate **Advisory & Forensics Consulting** engagements.
- [ ] Begin private beta of **IaaS / White-Label Index Builder**.

### Horizon 2 — Platform & Ecosystem Scaling (2027 – 2028)
*Builds the platform that turns TX into a multi-product organisation.*

- [ ] GA release of **Index-as-a-Service** platform (including marketplace).
- [ ] Integrate **Policy Simulation Tools** into the Policymaker API.
- [ ] Deploy **Ethical Middleware** SDK to first enterprise design systems.
- [ ] Launch **Whistleblower & Evidence Escrow** platform with legal partnerships.
- [ ] Expand **Sensor Network** to international deployments.

### Horizon 3 — Transformative Bets (2028+)
*Invests in the products that redefine categories and cement TX’s legacy.*

- [ ] Public release of **Digital Autonomy Consumer App**.
- [ ] Achieve first regulatory precedent using **Forensic Lab** evidence.
- [ ] Deploy **Democratic Governance Platform** for a national-level citizen assembly.
- [ ] Scale **Regulatory Submission Accelerator** to cover all major AI regulatory regimes.
- [ ] Institutionalise **Foundation** as a globally recognised research institute.

> Each product’s entry onto the roadmap is gated by the *XCIF Maturity Pre-Requisite Matrix* maintained in `/gates/`. No product proceeds past a gate without a passing score from the Peer-Review Board.

## 6. How to Use This Research

- **For the Transparency X Core Team:** Use this repo as the single source of truth for all strategic product discussions. All RFCs for new ventures should reference the relevant product canvas.
- **For Partners & Investors:** Review the Product Portfolio Map and Roadmap to understand how we build financial sustainability without compromising mission.

## 7. Contributing

We welcome strategic contributions just as we welcome code contributions.

1. **Propose a New Product Group:** Open an Issue using the `New Product Group Proposal` template, mapping it onto the Extender/Separate taxonomy and providing initial scoring.
2. **Critique an Existing Canvas:** Use Issues or PRs to refine market sizing, technical feasibility, or ethical risks for any product group.
3. **Become a Domain Reviewer:** If you have deep expertise in a relevant market (e.g., RegTech, forensic auditing, civic tech), request to join the Product Strategy Review Panel.

All contributions must align with the Transparency X Mission & Values (see `VALUES.md`).
